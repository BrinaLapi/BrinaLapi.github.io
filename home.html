Z igro sem se začela resno ukvarjati, ko sem obiskovala umetniško gimnazijo v Ljubljani - dramsko gledališka smer. V 4 letih mojega šolanja sem igrala v mnogih gledaliških predstavah, kasneje pa sem bila tudi del igralske ekipe pri Little Rooster Production. V tretjem letniku dramske šole sem v sodelovanju z Multimedijsko šolo dobila eno glavnih vlog v kratkem filmu Titova tretja želja in od takrat naprej se veliko bolj posvečam igri pred kamero. Za Amnesty International natečaj sem odigrala glavno vlogo v filmu Žig zveri. V istem letu sem glavno vlogo imela tudi v filmu z naslovom Dolg, ki je nastal v okviru Muvit/6x60 Filmskega maratona. Od takrat naprej dobivam manjše vlogice v slovenskih serijah in filmih in od časa do časa posnamem kakšno reklamo.


[eng]
My acting career started when I attended Art Gymnasium in Ljubljana - theatre studies. In 4 years of my education I played in many theatre performances and later on I was also a part of an acting team at the Little Rooster Production (theatre group in Slovenija). In the third year of Gymnasium, in cooperation with the Multimedia School, I got one of the main roles in the short film Tito's Third Wish and since then I have been much more dedicated to "screen acting". For the Amnesty International competition of short movies, I played the lead role in the film Mark of the Beast. In the same year I also starred in the film entitled Dolg (Debt), which was created under the Muvit/6x60 Film Marathon. Since then I have been getting smaller roles in Slovenian series and films and from time to time I do commercials.
